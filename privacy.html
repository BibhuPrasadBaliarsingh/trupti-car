We also take additional measures to comply with the applicable laws regarding the use of personal data obtained from you.

However, we cannot guarantee the security of any personal data or information you disclose online due to the nature of the Internet. Data Privacy Disclaimer

You confirm that you are an Indian resident and shall not access the Website or the goods and services offered on it if you are not an Indian resident.

Trupti Motors respects your personal sphere

When processing personal data, we attach great importance to protecting your privacy and ensuring that your data is secure. This privacy policy explains what data we process, why we process it, and how you can object to such processing.

We collect, process, and store personal data (including IP addresses) only as permitted by law or with your consent, such as when registering for an event, service, or creating an account on our Website.

What data do we collect?

We collect various types of information from users of our Website, including name, address, email, phone number, login credentials, financial or banking data, insurance information, geo-location data (if permitted), and transaction details carried out on or through our Website.

How do we collect data?

  • Directly from you: When you provide information on our Website or create an account or interact with Trupti Motors online or offline.
  • Automatically: As you navigate through the Website, we collect data using cookies and other tracking technologies.

Do we share your data with third parties?

We share your personal data with third parties only when permitted or authorized by law or when you have given consent — for example, when we share data with service providers for your benefit.

How long do we retain your personal data?

We retain your data as long as necessary for our business relationship or legitimate interest. In all other cases, we delete your data unless retention is required by law (e.g., contracts and invoices for 10 years as per legal requirements).

What data is processed when you use our Website?

You can visit our Website without providing personal information. Our servers automatically log each visit with technical details such as:

  • IP address of your device
  • GPS location (if permitted)
  • Date and time of access
  • Referring website and search term (if applicable)
  • Accessed file name and URL
  • Search queries used
  • Operating system and browser details
  • Device type (desktop or mobile)
  • Transmission protocol used

This data helps us ensure system security, analyze performance, and optimize our online services. IP addresses are also used for Website personalization.

Automated Data Collection Technologies

  • Cookies: Small text files stored on your device to optimize Website usability. You can disable cookies via browser settings, though some functions may not work properly.
  • Flash Cookies: Used for storing preferences and navigation data.
  • Web Beacons: Tiny graphics in emails or pages that track Website usage analytics.

Social Media and Third-Party Links

Our Website includes links to social networks and third-party platforms (Facebook, Twitter, LinkedIn, etc.). Once you click these links, this Privacy Policy no longer applies — the third party’s privacy policy governs instead.

Newsletter

You can subscribe to our newsletter using the “double opt-in” method. If you no longer wish to receive it, you can unsubscribe anytime through email or our Website.

Your Rights Regarding Personal Data

  • Right to access your stored data
  • Right to correction or deletion
  • Right to revoke consent for data processing

You may exercise these rights by contacting us via email or post. See our contact section for details.

Data Security

We employ suitable technical and organizational security measures to protect stored personal data against unauthorized access, loss, or manipulation. Our systems are regularly updated to maintain the highest level of data security.

Amendments to This Policy

Trupti Motors reserves the right to amend or supplement this Privacy Policy at any time in accordance with applicable laws. Please check this page regularly for updates.
